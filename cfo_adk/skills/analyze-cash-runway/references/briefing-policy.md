# CFO Briefing Policy

Lead with current cash, Base Case runway, reserve status, and the first material risk date.

Prioritize actions:

1. Prevent missed payroll, tax, debt, or critical supplier payments.
2. Defer non-critical spending before the reserve breach.
3. Accelerate collectible inflows with a decision deadline.
4. Prepare funding before projected cash depletion.
5. Request approval for uncertain items that materially change the forecast.

Do not provide accounting, tax, legal, or investment advice. Do not describe statistical forecasts as confirmed schedules.
