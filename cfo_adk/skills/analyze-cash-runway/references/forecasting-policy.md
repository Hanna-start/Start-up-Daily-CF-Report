# Forecasting Policy

- Current cash comes from reconciled latest account balances when available.
- Operating burn includes operating fixed and variable outflows. Exclude investing, financing, transfers, and opening balances.
- Minimum cash reserve equals six months of normalized operating expenses unless the supplied calculation states otherwise.
- Approved recurring items may be scheduled on expected dates. Unapproved candidates are not commitments.
- Base Case uses historical operating averages and approved recurring items.
- Downside Case reduces inflows and increases outflows. Upside Case increases inflows and reduces outflows.
- Cash runway is current cash divided by monthly net cash burn when net burn is positive.
- Treat projected reserve breach and cash depletion dates as estimates, not contractual dates.
